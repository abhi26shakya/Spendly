Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and login credentials, along with the expense records you enter — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We may also collect basic usage information, such as pages visited and actions taken, to help us understand how the Service is used.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data you provide to operate the Service: authenticating your account, storing and displaying your expense records, and generating the summaries and breakdowns you see in the app. We may also use aggregated, non-identifying usage data to improve Spendly's features and reliability. We do not use your financial data for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored in our database and retained for as long as your account remains active. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. If you delete your account, your data will be removed from our active systems within a reasonable period.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal or financial data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may rely on trusted third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting and font services) to operate the Service, and these providers only process data as necessary to deliver their service to us.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, you can reach out to us through your account settings or the support channels listed on the Spendly website.
